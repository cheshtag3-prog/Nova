About Us

Nova is a full-service interior design and lifestyle agency dedicated to crafting sophisticated spaces that embody elegance and comfort.

We blend modern design principles with timeless aesthetics to create interiors that feel personal, luxurious, and purposeful.

Nova is an interior design and lifestyle agency focused on creating thoughtful, beautiful, and functional spaces.

At Nova, we believe that design is more than aesthetics — it’s about creating spaces that inspire the way you live.

Our approach combines creativity, thoughtful planning, and attention to detail.

“Designing spaces that feel like home — elevated, intentional, and timeless.”

Creative Director

At the heart of Nova is our Creative Director, whose vision shapes every project we undertake. With a deep passion for design and a refined eye for detail, they lead the creative direction of each space — ensuring it reflects elegance, functionality, and individuality.

Blending innovation with timeless aesthetics, the Creative Director works closely with clients to translate ideas into meaningful, beautifully curated environments.

Their commitment to excellence and thoughtful design defines Nova’s signature style.

Leader Designer

Nova’s Lead Designer plays a pivotal role in transforming concepts into beautifully executed spaces. With a strong sense of spatial planning and a refined design perspective, they ensure every project balances creativity with functionality.

From selecting materials and color palettes to overseeing layouts and styling details, the Lead Designer brings cohesion and character to each interior. Their collaborative approach ensures that every design not only looks exceptional but also enhances the way clients live and experience their spaces.

Our Lead Designer combines innovation and precision to craft interiors that are both visually striking and thoughtfully functional.
