General

At Nova, we specialize in residential interior design, space planning, renovation, and lifestyle styling. Our expertise lies in creating refined, functional, and timeless interiors that reflect our clients’ personalities and enhance everyday living.

Client satisfaction is our top priority. We maintain transparent communication throughout the project and encourage feedback at every stage. If concerns arise, we work collaboratively to resolve them before moving forward, ensuring you feel confident and comfortable with the design direction.

We believe clear and consistent communication is key to a successful project. We stay connected through scheduled meetings, calls, emails, and progress updates. Clients are informed at every major milestone to ensure alignment and transparency.

Nova works with a trusted network of skilled contractors and vendors when required. All collaborators are carefully selected to maintain our quality standards and ensure seamless project execution under our supervision.
